# Bush Lab — Biophysical Mass Spectrometry @ UW

[biophysicalms.org](https://biophysicalms.org) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [UW Department of Chemistry](https://chem.washington.edu) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [@bushlab.bsky.social](https://bsky.app/profile/bushlab.bsky.social)

---

The Bush Lab develops and applies **mass spectrometry and ion mobility spectrometry** techniques to elucidate the structures, interactions, and assembly of protein complexes and subcellular machines. We are part of the [Department of Chemistry](https://chem.washington.edu) and the [Biological Physics, Structure & Design Program](https://depts.washington.edu/bpsd/) at the [University of Washington](https://www.washington.edu/).

Our approach combines custom-built instrumentation, experimental biophysics, and computational methods to study biological systems at the intersection of chemistry, physics, and biology.

→ For a full overview of our research, people, and publications, visit **[biophysicalms.org](https://biophysicalms.org)**.
